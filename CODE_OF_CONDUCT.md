# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal
appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and empathetic
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing private information without permission
- Any conduct that could be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards.
Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent bans.

## Reporting

Report incidents privately via repository maintainers.
